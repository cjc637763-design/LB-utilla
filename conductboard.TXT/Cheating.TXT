CHEATING CAN INCLUDE, BUT IS NOT LIMITED TO:
- USING MODS OUTSIDE OF MODDED ROOMS THAT HAVE THE ABILITY TO MODIFY GAMEPLAY IN THE ROOM
- USING EXPLOITS OR MODIFICATIONS THAT NEGATIVELY IMPACT GAMEPLAY IN ANY ROOM

YOU CAN POTENTIALLY RECEIVE A BAN FROM CHEATING IN A FEW WAYS:
- YOU CAN BE REPORTED BY OTHER PLAYERS (INCLUDING IN MODDED ROOMS), WHICH MAY LEAD TO ACTION IF A PATTERN OF REPORTS OCCURS
- THE ANTI-CHEAT SYSTEM MAY TAKE ACTION IF ATTEMPTED CHEATING IS DETECTED

TO REDUCE THE RISK OF A BAN AND MOD MORE SAFELY:
- USE TRUSTED SOURCES SUCH AS REPUTABLE MODDING COMMUNITIES
- BE CAUTIOUS OF MODS THAT ORIGINATE FROM CHEAT COMMUNITIES OR INCLUDE OPTIONS LABELED AS "DETECTED"

ULTIMATELY, IT IS UP TO YOU, THE USER, TO DECIDE HOW YOU CHOOSE TO MOD AND WHAT RISKS YOU ARE WILLING TO TAKE.
